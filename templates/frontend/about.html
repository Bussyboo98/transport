What Is Driving Us

  • Safety for everyone – We are redefining public travel experience by ensuring that you are safe throughout your commuting time with us using technology and automations.

  • Excellent shared commuting service – We provide you with hassle free and smart bus travel. You can book a ride and schedule a trip right from the comfort of your home. Our professional pilots and air-conditioned buses are available 24/7.

  • Connect people –A ride with us is an opportunity to meet new people in your community and build defining and important relationships.

  • Affordable Ride – We provide an on demand buspool trip at the cheapest price you can ever imagined with professional drivers and air-conditioned buses.

  • Save the planet – By helping to reduce the number of cars on the road, we can save the planet and make it healthier than we met it.

Meet our Founders
